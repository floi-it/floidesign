Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

This site (floi.it) is run by Francesca Loi, an independent designer based in Italy. You can reach me at floidesignart@gmail.com.

What we collect

If you accept analytics, this site uses PostHog to record:

  • Pages you visit and the order you visit them
  • Your approximate location (country, city — derived from IP, IP itself is not stored)
  • Browser type, screen size, and device type
  • Anonymized session replays of mouse movement, clicks, and scrolls — form inputs are masked

If you decline, none of this is collected. The site does not use any other tracking — no Google Analytics, no Facebook pixel, no advertising cookies.

Why we collect it

To understand how visitors use the site, find broken pages, and improve the design. Data is never sold or shared with advertisers.

Where it goes

Data is stored on PostHog's EU servers. PostHog is GDPR-compliant. PostHog privacy policy.

How long we keep it

Session replays: 30 days. Aggregate analytics: 1 year. After that, data is automatically deleted.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Ask what data we have about you
  • Ask us to delete it
  • Withdraw your consent at any time (button below)

To make any of these requests, email floidesignart@gmail.com.
