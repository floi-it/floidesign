Hi, I'm Francesca

...but you can call me floi!

I enjoy working on product design, graphics, and Webflow projects. I strongly believe what makes a designer is a good sense of observation, analysis and insights.
Most of my free time is spent trying to make my 3d printer work or trying to fix something around the house, but sometimes I just need a walk outside.
I'm a lifelong planner, inconsistent reader, and good fork. I love travelling, and taking pictures of wild animals and birds. Sometimes I admit, it's only my family's cats.

You can check out my work on Instagram, or Behance. To get in touch and get surely an answer, drop me a line.
